About Me

  I am Vennjy Lugo, A 19-year-old Filipino who is a sophomore at Ateneo de Davao University studying computer engineering for the year 2023-2024. I started programming when I was 17 years old, though I mostly just fooled around at the time, not taking it seriously. But when I successfully ran my first program (it was a "hello world" written in python), I fell in love with programming. It was then when I confidently decided to myself, this is going to be my career. Afterwards it became my hobby.


WebDev Journey

  I started to learn website development a year later, though its mostly front-end stuff. I scowered the internet for resources for learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, then I applied my knowledge by creating dummy websites. I eventually stumbled upon bootstrap which saved me so much time for the looks and feel for my website, and thus I began using it frequently. Even now.
